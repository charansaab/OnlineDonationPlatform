About Us

We are working for underprivileged communities with the courage to provide them with better living, education and equal rights. This website is purposely built to help poor people all over India. It will motivate the people who directly want to donate their charitable to needy people. It is India's first platform which provides equal rights and chances to everyone to donate whatever they want according to their status and make sure the charitable are going to the right persons.
We are working to involve more people and motivate them to help other people. Our main purpose is to provide a platform to donors and needy people so that they both come across and share things that they can donate and the needy can benefit from. This platform will also promote NGOs which are struggling and striving to get donations for their purposes.

Mission

My mission was to develop an open-source platform where people can directly find the things they need for free. It will kind a donation marketplace which will provide all kinds of charitable.

Vision

To see more people join the platform and encourage them to donate more according to their wishes. A vision and hope to lessen the poverty rate and fulfil the needs of underprivileged people.

What We Do

We are providing an open-source marketplace for posting charitable content which everyone can access equally and have an equal chance to get it. People can upload their households according desire.
